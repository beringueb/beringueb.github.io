Rayleigh scattering of the CMB.

Rayleigh scattering is the extra scattering of CMB photons by neutral species formed during recombination. Thanks to this effect, the last scattering surface becomes frequency-dependent. In a series of three publications, we have studied the cosmological implications of this effect as well as its detectability with the next generation of CMB surveys.

Component separation for the Simons Observatory : sps4lat.

Component separation refers to the complex problem of disentangling signals of interest in a noisy mixture. sps4lat is a proposed component separation method for Simons Observatory.
