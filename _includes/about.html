About me

Hello ! I am cosmologist, working at APC (AstroParticule et Cosmologie) with Professor Josquin Errard. Before that, I was a postdoctoral research associate at Cardiff Univerity, with Professor Erminia Calabrese. I am observing the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB), the left-over radiation from the Big-Bang, to better understand the origin and content of our Universe. I am a member of the ACT , Simons Observatory (SO) and CCAT-prime collaborations.
